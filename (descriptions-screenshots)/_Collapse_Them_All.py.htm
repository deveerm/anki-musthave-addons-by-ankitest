1846969611 Expand and Collapse Decks Tree in Browser A simple add-on that collapses the Tree of Decks in the Browser window. Creates two options in Go menu to expand and collapse all items (Favourites searches, Decks and Tags) in Browse window.
  • Just press the Ctrl+Shift+Minus to collapse
  • and Ctrl+Shift+Plus keys to expand and view sub-decks.
