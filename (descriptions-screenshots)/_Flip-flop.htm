519426347 This is very simple add-on. You can easily flip-flop FrontSide / BackSide with 0 (Zero) or Insert key. Use Ctrl+PageUp, Ctrl+9 or F7 to look at FrontSide of your currently visible card. Use Control+PageDown, Control+3 or F8 to look at BackSide of your currently visible card.
